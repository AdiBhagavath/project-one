## INSTACRYPTO vs Other exchanges(CoinDCX-Binance)
# Format
Simple beginner friendly, fast and simple kyc
# Feature 
Direct crypto exchange buy/sell, no simple/complex trading features
# Custody 
non-custodial platform, buy or sell directly from external wallet, full user control unlike coindcx or binance which are custodial funds and assets on their platform in their control.
# Fees 
Low platform fees, lower sell fees
# Currency/coins 
InstaCrypto:multiple cryptocurrency , for Indians, supports INR
CoinDCX: multiple cryptocurrency, trading features, for Indians, supports INR 
Binance:Limited cryptocurrency to buy in india, complex trading options, global support, multiple fiat/local currency support.
